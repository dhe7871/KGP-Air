Introduction

A student group project to monitor and collect the Pollution Data using Fixed Wing Drone over and around the Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur campus. This Project is a part of Flight Testing Laboratory course conducted by the Department of Aerospace Engineering, IIT Kharagpur and taken by Prof. Sandeep Saha.

Sensors Used

This project uses variety of sensors like PMS7003, CO2 Sensor, Neo-M8N GPS Sensor, DFRobot's CO and NO2 sensors etc.
